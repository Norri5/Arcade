Super Mario

Super Mario Bros was released in 1985 for the Nintendo Entertainment System. It featured Mario and Luigi trying to rescue Princess Toadstool from Bowser. The side-scrolling platform game was an instant classic and helped launch the popularity of the Super Mario series.


Pac Man

Pac-Man was released in 1980 as an arcade game by Namco. Players guide the titular yellow character around a maze, eating pellets while avoiding ghosts. With its simple yet addictive gameplay, Pac-Man became an iconic classic and one of the most successful video games of all time.


Street Fighter

Street Fighter is a fighting game franchise first released by Capcom in 1987. Players select from a diverse roster of characters, each with their own unique moves and fighting styles. The Street Fighter series pioneered many gameplay innovations for the genre and remains one of the most popular and influential fighting game franchises.
